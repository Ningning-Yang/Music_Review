Scars to your beautiful

The Canadian singer Alessia Cara comes back with a strong song Scars to your beautiful from Know It All Album. In this song, Alessia gives a message to us all by saying no scars to your beautiful. Everyone is beautiful but, unfortunately, not everyone knows the beauty deep in them!

She do not see her perfect, she do not understand she is worth it. This line is for everyone to know that they do not need to change anything and that their beauty lies in their confidence, love, compassion, respect, etc.

'And you do not have to change a thing'

'The world could change its heart'

'No scars to your beautiful, we are stars and we are beautiful'

Rate: ★★★★★
