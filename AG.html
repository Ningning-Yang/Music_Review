The Good Parts

Not surprisingly, the American singer Andy Grammer comes back to us with a great song called The Good Parts from The Good Parts Album. It is clear that Andy is trying to show us a great concept by making his song name the same as the album name! The Good Parts song reminds us to not forget the good parts even in our difficulties!

Grammer explained why he titled his album after this song by saying If someone does a memoir, there is a piece of the memoir that everybody really wants to hear about the good parts, that is what we want. I want you to tell me what it is to be alive as you”.
