Creep

“Creep,” Radiohead’s 1992 anthem of alienation, is one of those songs that everyone has loved at some point, and no one would admit to loving now. It’s hard to watch the original music video without cringing a little bit.

The feeling when you feel nothing but love for someone, and they call you a creep is the worst feeling on earth esides getting a real painful operation. It is a cause of major depression, when you go through it and everyone does. The song is nothing but the goods.

"You want me to notice when you’re not around…

You wish you were special…

But you’re a creep… You don’t belong here."

Rate: ★★★★★
